Award/Task Creator

Many students struggle to decide which assignment to work on for their classes. In this feature, you can submit tasks, and they will be listed for you. When you're ready to choose a task, it will randomly select one from the list in a dynamic display and then finalize the selection for you. Once you complete a task, it will show that you have finished it. After you complete a certain number of tasks, an icon will appear to award you for your work.

Pomodoro Timer

One of the best strategies for completing your assignments is dedicating time to focus and taking small breaks. Using the Pomodoro Timer, you can set a fixed time for studying, leave the extension running on your computer, and when the timer finishes, it will remind you with an alert sound.

Focus Accomidation

In a world where everything is competing for your attention, it’s difficult to focus on your assignments. We created Focus Accommodation to help you eliminate some distractions on your computer. You can choose websites that easily distract you, causing them to lose their volume and blur the screen. You can choose to limit websites like Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and other sites of your choice.

Site Reminder

A common problem for students is turning in assignments late or forgetting about them. With the Assignment Reminder, you'll be notified about your upcoming assignments the next time you open the app, ensuring you don’t forget.
